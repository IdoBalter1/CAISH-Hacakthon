Convert SVG to PNG for iOS

To convert the icon.svg to PNG for iOS:

  1. Open icon.svg in a browser (drag and drop the file)
  2. Take a screenshot or use browser DevTools to export
  3. Or use an online tool: CloudConvert
  4. Export as 1024x1024 PNG
  5. Save as: ios/App/App/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/AppIcon-512@2x.png

Or use this quick method:

  1. Open icon.svg in Safari/Chrome
  2. Right-click > Inspect Element
  3. Set viewBox to 1024x1024 in the SVG
  4. Take screenshot at 1024x1024
